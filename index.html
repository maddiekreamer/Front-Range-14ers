Front Range 14ers

Hiking at 14,000ft is fun, but it needs to be done safely.

Front Range 14ers is here to help you check the weather to ensure a fun and safe experience. Alpine starts (pre-dawn starts) are recommended, as these climbs can take 7-8+ hours and storms typically hit in the afternoon. Try to get to treeline by noon. As a rule of thumb, a 20% or below chance of precip is a pretty good bet you'll have a clear afternoon. Click on the image of the mountain you are interested in climbing to check the forecast and see if it is safe to hike!
